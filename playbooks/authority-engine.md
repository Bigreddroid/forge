# Authority Engine Playbook

Turn your expertise into undeniable category leadership.

## Goal

Become the founder people quote, reference, and seek out — before they ever think about hiring someone else.

## Step 1: Map Your Authority Territory

Answer these four questions to define the territory you will own:

1. **Who do you serve?** (be specific — not "founders", but "B2B SaaS founders under $5M ARR")
2. **What problem do you solve better than anyone?** (the one thing you could talk about for 10 hours)
3. **What does everyone in your space get wrong?** (your contrarian angle)
4. **What outcome do you promise?** (specific, measurable, believable)

**Output:** A one-sentence authority statement.

> Example: "I help B2B SaaS founders build content systems that generate $50K+ in inbound pipeline per month — without a marketing team."

## Step 2: Build Your Signature Framework

Every category leader has a named methodology. Create yours.

**Framework formula:**
- Give it a name (acronym, metaphor, or invented word)
- Define 3-5 stages or components
- Make it visual (a simple diagram)
- Use it in every piece of content

**Example:** The FORGE System — Authority → Content → Distribution → Leads → Revenue → Intelligence

## Step 3: Define Your Content Pillars

Pick 3 content pillars that map to your ICP's journey:

| Pillar | Purpose | Example |
|--------|---------|---------|
| **Education** | Build trust, give value | "How to build a content system in 30 days" |
| **Opinion** | Differentiate, attract your people | "Why personal branding advice is killing founders" |
| **Proof** | Convert, build credibility | "How [client] went from 0 to 50 inbound leads/month" |

## Step 4: Establish Your Voice

Train your AI (FORGE Voice Agent) using these inputs:
- 20-30 examples of your best writing
- Your word blacklist (words you never use)
- Your tone spectrum (casual ↔ formal, humble ↔ bold)
- Your go-to analogies and metaphors

See `prompts/voice-training/founder-voice.md` for the prompt template.

## Step 5: Own One Platform First

Don't spread thin. Pick one platform, dominate it, then expand.

**Recommended order for B2B founders:**
1. LinkedIn (highest ICP density, longest content shelf life)
2. Newsletter (owned audience, compounding over time)
3. Twitter/X (real-time credibility, journalist/VC attention)
4. YouTube (highest trust, lowest competition among founders)
5. Podcast (deepest relationship, niche authority)
